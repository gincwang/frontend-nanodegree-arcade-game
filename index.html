How to play:
Use Space Bar to select/continue/unpause the game.
Use your directional keys to navigate the game level:
- if you are hit by the ladybugs, you lose a life point
- if you walk over the gem, you score a game point
- The game will end once you run out of life points
Collect as many gems as you can to get your high score! Good Luck!!
