How to play:
  • Use "Space Bar/Enter" keys for navigation.
  • Use "Directional keys" to control your character.

Game Rules:
  • You start with 3 hearts(HP).
  • Getting hit by ladybug will cost you 1 heart
    (Gotta press "space/enter" again after all the ladybugs are off the screen to resume playing).
  • Walk over the gem to collect your gem point.
  • The game will end once you run out of hearts(HP).
  • Collect as many gems as you can to get your high score! Good Luck!!
